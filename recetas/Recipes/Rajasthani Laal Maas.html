Rajasthani Laal Maas

No Testeado, Principal, South Asia

Ingredientes

Ingredients for Rajasthani Laal Maas:


- Tender Goat meat with bones- 500 gms

- Ghee (or Mustard Oil) - 4 tbsp

- Onion, fine sliced- 2 medium

- Ginger garlic paste- 1 tbsp

- Rajasthani Mathania Red Chillies- 5 nos. ( if not available, can use Kashmiri Red Chillies)

- Rajasthani Kachri powder - 1 tbsp (optional)

- Coriander powder-2 tsp

- Garam Masala powder- 3/4th tsp

- Curd or Yogurt whisked- 3 tbsp

- Salt- 1/2 tsp


Whole garam masala & spices:

- Green cardamom- 4

- Cloves-6

- Cinnamon-3

- Black cardamom- 2

- Bay leaf- 2

Instrucciones

Preparation:

- Soak the Mathania red chillies in warm water for around 30 mins. Once soft, make a paste adding 2 tbsp water.
- Fine slice the onions.
- Whisk the curd

Process:

- Heat ghee or mustard oil.
- Add the whole garam masalas and bay leaf & give it a stir.
- Add the sliced onions and fry on medium heat for 5 mins till light brown.
- Add the ginger garlic paste and fry on low for 2 mins till raw smell is gone.
- Now put in the pieces of meat, mix well and fry on medium heat for around 4 mins till it is browned.
- Add the salt & kachri Powder, mix & fry for around 3 mins on low.
- Now add the coriander powder, mix & bhunno on medium to low well for around 10 mins.
- Add the red Chilli paste, mix & bhunno well for around 5 mins till oil separates & the Chilli paste is cooked.
- Reduce heat to low & add the whisked curd. Mix well & cook for 2-3 mins stirring continuously. Let it cook till oil separates.
- Add water, mix , cover & cook for around 20 mins on low heat.
- Add garam masala powder, mix and cover & cook for 10 mins or till meat is tender.
- In case you haven’t added Kachri powder (meat tenderizer), pressure cook in the end till 4 whistles .
