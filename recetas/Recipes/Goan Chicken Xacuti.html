Goan Chicken Xacuti

No Testeado, Principal, South Asia

Fuente: M.facebook.com

Ingredientes

marination

1 kg chicken

1/2 tsp of tumeric powder

2 tsp of chilli powder

1 tsp or more lime juice

3-4 tbsp green paste(ginger garlic , coriander

leaves & green chilli)

1 1/2 Onion (sliced)

1/2 cup Freshly grated coconut

1/2 tsp turmeric powder

10 cloves

2 inch Cinnamon stick

2 Green Cardamom

1 tsp Black Pepper Corns

1/2 tbsp White Poppy Seeds

1/4 Poppy flower (Star Anise)

1/2 Mace

1/4 tsp Star-anise

1 tbsp Corainder seeds

Pinch Nutmeg

5 -6 Dry Kashmiri Chillies

Instrucciones

salt to season the meat

let the chicken marinate for 30 minutes

minimum.

fry onion & coconut in cooking oil until fragrant.

add 1/2 tsp of tumeric powder.

Separately dry roast above mentioned whole

spices .

grind onion coconut and dry roasted spices into

a smooth thick paste. add very little water to

make a paste.

making of the curry

heat the pot and add 1-2 finely chopped

onions and 1finely chopped tomato.

fry until soft, then add the marinated chicken.

fry the chicken for 5-6 minutes on medium-high

flame •add the grinded curry paste and let it

simmer for good 15-20 minutes.

add salt as per taste (remember the chicken

already has salt so add accordingly)

turn off the flame and add some coriander

leaves roughly chopped for garnishing.
