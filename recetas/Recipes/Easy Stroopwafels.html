Easy Stroopwafels

★★★

Europe, Merienda, Pastelería, Testeado

Tiempo de preparación: 15 mins Tiempo de Cocción: 25 mins Porciones: 10 servings Fuente: Toineskitchen.com

Ingredientes

Waffles

245 g All-purpose flour

245g ≈ 2 cups + 1tbsp of all-purpose flour

10 g Instant yeast

10g ≈ 3.5 teaspoons of yeast

70 g Superfine sugar

70f ≈ ⅓ cup of superfine sugar

120 g Soft butter

120g ≈ 8.5tbsp

15 ml Lukewarm milk

15ml ≈ 1tbsp

1 Egg

0.50 tsp Ground cinnamon

2 g salt

Syrup

100 g Brown sugar

100g ≈ ½ cup of brown sugar

175 g Keukenstroop

Or use 117g of golden syrup and 58g of treacle (black strap molasses)

75 g Unsalted butter

(cambiamos a 150 azucar, 50 miel, 75 manteca y canela)

75g ≈ 5tbsp + 1 tsp of butter

1 tsp Ground cinnamon

1 pinch Salt

10cm (4") cookie cutter

Instrucciones

In the stand mixer with the paddle attachment, cream together the butter, cinnamon, and sugar. You may have to scrape down the sides of the bowl.

Once creamed, add the milk, and yeast, and combine.

Scrape down the sides of the bowl, then add the egg, and let the mixer run for another minute or so.

While the mixer is running, mix the salt through the flour.

Add the flour, and let it mix, until no dough is sticking to the sides of the mixing bowl anymore.

On a floured surface, knead the dough for a few minutes.

Cut the dough into 50g balls, and put them on a cookie sheet. Cover with cling film, and let them sit in a warm spot for at least 45 minutes, up to an hour.

In a heavy-bottomed saucepan, over medium-low heat, mix all the syrup ingredients together, and keep stirring until the butter and sugar are melted, and there are no visible sugar crystals. For me, I can tell it's done when the syrup hits a gentle boil (just a few bubbles around the edges of the pan).

When done, turn off the heat.

Put a glass bowl over a pan with simmering water, and pour the syrup in the bowl. It'll keep the syrup liquid enough to spread, without the risk of burning the sugar.
