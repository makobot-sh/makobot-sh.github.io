Pandhra Rassa - पांढरा रस्सा | Kolhapuri Style Chicken Curry | Recipe by Archana in Marathi

Fuente: M.youtube.com

Ingredientes

Ingredients:


250 gms Chicken

2 tbsp Oil

3 Dry Red Chillies

1 Onion, chopped

1 tsp Masala [Cinnamon (dalchini), Caraway Seeds (shahjeera), Cloves (lavang), Coriander seeds (dhane) ]

1 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste

1/2 tsp White Pepper Powder

2 tbsp Cashew - Khaskhas - Dried Coconut paste

1 Cup Coconut Milk

Salt to taste

1 Cup Water

Instrucciones

Method:

- In a pan, add oil and dry red chillies.
- Then add onion, masala (cinnamon, caraway seeds, cloves, coriander seeds), salt and mix well.
- Add ginger garlic paste, white pepper powder, chicken & mix everything well.
- Then add cashew - khaskhas - dried coconut paste and mix well.
- Add coconut milk and again mix everything well.
- Add some water and let the gravy boil on a low flame for 5 to 7 minutes.
- Spicy Hot Pandhra Rassa is ready.
